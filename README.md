# Social decision making journal club｜社会决策读书会

## Background of the journal club | 读书会背景

Dr. Michael Hallquist, director of the Developmental Personality Neuroscience Laboratory (DEPENd Lab) at UNC-Chapel Hill, provided this reading list for his graduate student, Ruofan Ma, to orient her to computational modeling and social decision making research in the DEPENd Lab. Given this context, the list inevitably has closer proximity to research in the DEPENd Lab than the broader field of social decision-making or decision neuroscience. Please visit the DEPENd lab website for more information about research in the lab: https://dependlab.unc.edu/

A journal club started by Ruofan meets twice a week to discuss readings on this list. The language of presentation is Mandarin Chinese, unless otherwise specified.
